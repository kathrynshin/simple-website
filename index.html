Veritas

Our Mission

Welcome to Veritas, a free resource designed to help students master Latin vocabulary, grammar, and translation skills.

Who we are

We are an organization aiming to promote the study of classics in our community by supporting students involved in the National Junior Classical League, the National Latin Exam, and Certamen. For students our goal is to provide a chatbot for them to get consistent and independent practice, with progress being tracked and resources being stored in one place.

Why Studying Classics is Important

We believe that studying the classics is crucial to understanding our world today. While students indeed are receiving practice to complete exams, these are lessons that can apply beyond Latin competitions. When we study the past, we realize that the roman empire that ruled 2000 years ago is not much different from our society today. Ideas of political corruption and civic identity are not new. Both middleschool and highschool are a crucial time for students to pursue their passion for classics but also prepare themselves for the NJCL exams, NLE, and Certmamen. We recognize that these exams are highly competitive and since students across the country participate in these events. Therefore, it can be intimidating to know where to start, find resources that are in one place and get consistent practice
