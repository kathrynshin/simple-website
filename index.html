Veritas

Our Mission

Welcome to Veritas, a free resource designed to help students master Latin vocabulary, grammar, and translation skills.

Who We Are

We are an organization aiming to promote the study of classics in our community by supporting students involved in the National Junior Classical League, the National Latin Exam, and Certamen. For students, our goal is to provide a chatbot for consistent and independent practice, with progress tracked and resources stored in one place.

Why Studying Classics Is Important

We believe that studying the classics is crucial to understanding our world today. While students are receiving practice to complete exams, these are lessons that can apply beyond Latin competitions. When we study the past, we realize that the Roman Empire that ruled 2,000 years ago is not much different from our society today.

Both middle school and high school are crucial times for students to pursue their passion for classics while also preparing for the NJCL exams, NLE, and Certamen.
