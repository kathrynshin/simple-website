About Us

Kathryn Shin:Growing up in the classics community, Kathryn has been involved in NJJCL and the NLE since eighth grade. In the past she has entered many of these exams and competed in rounds of Certamen. After winning the regional Certamen competition at Princeton University twice and earning a gold on the NLE exam, she realized the importance of having resources located in one place, and a resource that would give independent practice daily so she did not have to create new quizlets and resources for herself every time and need someone to quiz her every time. Especially with pressure on her to compete against other people, finding the time to create her own quizlets, find practice resources, and someone to practice with was very difficult. While there are definitely preparatory packets and past exams to better your skills for these competitions, there is no standard resource that holds all of them in one place.
