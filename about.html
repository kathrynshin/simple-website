About Us

Kathryn Shin

Growing up in the classics community, Kathryn has been involved in NJJCL and the NLE since eighth grade. In the past, she has entered many of these exams and competed in rounds of Certamen. After winning the regional Certamen competition at Princeton University twice and earning gold on the NLE exam, she realized the importance of having resources located in one place.

She wanted a resource that would give students independent daily practice, so they would not have to create new Quizlets and resources for themselves every time or need someone to quiz them. Especially with the pressure of competing against other people, finding time to create resources, find practice materials, and practice consistently can be very difficult.

While there are preparatory packets and past exams that help students improve their skills, there is no standard resource that holds all of them in one organized place.
